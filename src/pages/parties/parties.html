Swedish Party System

Although there are 8 parties currently represented in the legislature, all but one belongs to a coalition bloc.

These coalition blocs are divided into the left-leaning "Red-Greens" and the conservative "Alliance" with the far-right Sweden Democrats not belonging to any bloc.


The "Red-Greens" are led by the Social Democrats with the Green Party as another primary member along with tacit support from the Left Party.


The "Alliance" is led by the Moderate Party with the Christian Democrats, Centre, and Liberal parties as other members.
